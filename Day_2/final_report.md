### 1. Executive Summary  
Saudi Vision 2030 has positioned artificial intelligence (AI) as a core pillar for economic diversification, digital transformation, and global competitiveness. The Kingdom is investing heavily in digital infrastructure, human capital, and a vertically‑integrated AI ecosystem that spans public services, industry, and sovereign data platforms. Projections indicate that AI will contribute tens of billions of dollars to the GDP by 2030, underpinning the shift away from oil dependence.  

Key take‑aways:  
- AI is no longer a peripheral technology; it is a strategic engine for Vision 2030.  
- The market is expected to reach **USD 16.9 billion by 2032** (CAGR driven by Vision 2030).  
- Major growth drivers include generative AI, Arabic‑centric large‑language models, and AI‑enabled marketing & sales.  
- The government’s “Sovereign AI” and “Cloud 3.0” initiatives create a demand for secure, low‑latency infrastructure and data‑governance solutions.  
- Enterprise opportunities span AI‑as‑a‑Service, sector‑specific AI, talent development, and cross‑border partnerships.

   - Develop clear AI ethics guidelines, data‑governance policies, and transparency mechanisms to build public trust.
